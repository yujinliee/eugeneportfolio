Accessible Marketplace for PWDs

Solo Project — 2026

Given a case study brief to design and prototype an accessible desktop web marketplace (1440×1080px) in Figma for persons with disabilities (PWDs) to browse and purchase mobility aids and daily living tools. The design focused on high contrast, legible typography, clear layout hierarchy, and intuitive navigation — covering two main categories: Mobility Aids (wheelchairs, canes, scooters) and Daily Living Tools (kitchenware, bathing aids, medication organizers), organized by task and room for easy discovery.
